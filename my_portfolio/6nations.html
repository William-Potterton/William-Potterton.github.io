6 nations insights

Investigating the the 6 nations rugby tournament over a 3 year period and creating insights from this data.

Objective

To better understand the data behind Ireland's back to back championship wins, by comparing against the other teams and also on an individual basis. Using key statistics and a combination of 2023 and 2024, great insights were gained on Ireland rugby's success.

PowerBi Workspace

Exploring the dataset through PowerBi's visual dashboards.

Tools

Dataset

Sourced originally from sixnationsrugby, provided information on key statistics such as, time played, tackles and metres gained.
