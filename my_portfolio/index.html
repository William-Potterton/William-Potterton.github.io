About Me

My life journey

I am a motivated individual entering the data analytics industry after completing a bachelor’s degree in business and analytics.

Having previously had a short career in the cheffing and restaurant industry, I gained many insights into the importance of working in a team, time management and multi-tasking, especially for problem-solving.

When the coronavirus shut down much of the hospitality industry in 2020, I knew I needed to change careers and as someone who has always had a strong affiliation with mathematics and business, I pivoted to studying business studies at DCU.

While at DCU I have gained experience in roles such as sales, accounting and business development for a tech startup. With a consistent love of playing, watching and studying every little detail of sports, I was drawn to the business analytics speciality for final year. This began my data analytics journey.

DCU has provided me the opportunity to explore much of the data analytics industry, allowing me to gain key skills in tools such as PowerBi, Python, SQL and 3D modelling platform Blender. Furthermore, by working on multiple projects in groups and as an individual, such as Feature Proposal, I have learned much about the emphasis of collaboration and problem-solving in the analytics industry.

I have a passion for sports and therefore many of my career goals are focused on making an impact with data analytics on the sports industry. Taking inspiration from many leading analysts in the sport industry and having much of my sporting success coming from rugby, the hope would be to working there in years to come. My short-term goals are to gain experience in a data analytics role and continue to learn and expand my portfolio, fuelled by my interests in analytics.

For further information about me see My Resume. Feel free to connect or contact me via LinkedIn.
